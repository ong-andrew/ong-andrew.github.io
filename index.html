About Me

Hi, I’m Andrew. I believe journalism matters because it holds power to account — but today, ad dollars are dwindling and people’s attention is harder than ever to keep. That’s why my mission is to help newsrooms adapt, using technology not for its own sake but to deepen relationships with audiences and stay relevant to the communities they serve.

As a father, I often think about the future we’re shaping, and that drives me to keep learning and improving. Whether it’s experimenting with new tools, rethinking how stories are told, or streamlining newsroom workflows, I’m always looking for ways to make journalism more sustainable and meaningful in a changing world.
